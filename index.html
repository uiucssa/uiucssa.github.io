About

The Singapore Students Association at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign strives to provide our community of over 120 Singaporean undergraduates, graduates, postgraduates, and alumnus a home away from home.

Regular get-together makan sessions are arranged for all members, and non-Singaporeans are also welcomed!

For our international friends, "makan" is the Malay word for "eat", which has been absorbed into the national interlingual dialect known as "Singlish".
